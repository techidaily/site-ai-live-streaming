## Part 2\. How To Live Stream to Instagram With an RTMP?

Setting high-quality live streams encourages people to tune into your broadcast. For this purpose, you need a seamless encoder like [OBS](https://obsproject.com/). Unfortunately, Instagram doesn't officially support third-party encoders. To solve this query, you need an effective solution.

Therefore, the following steps guide streaming on Instagram with an RTMP (Real-Time Messaging Protocol). You will also learn how to set the required parameters in this comprehensive guide:

* **Step 1.** Start with downloading and launching OBS Studio on your PC. The first thing to do is set up vertical camera dimensions for **RTMP Instagram live** streaming. To access the settings window, hit the ‚ÄúSettings‚Äù option in the ‚ÄúControls‚Äù panel.

![proceed to settings in obs](https://images.wondershare.com/virbo/article/2024/03/guide-to-stream-to-instagram-with-rtmp-2.jpg)

* **Step 2.** Look for the ‚ÄúVideo‚Äù tab in the ‚ÄúSettings‚Äù window that opens. After that, enter ‚Äú1080x1920‚Äù in the ‚ÄúBase (Canvas) Resolution‚Äù bar. Similarly, add a ‚Äú1080x1920‚Äù value in the ‚ÄúOutput (Scaled) Resolution‚Äù bar to set a 9:16 aspect ratio for vertical display. Then, hit the ‚ÄúOK‚Äù button to apply these changes to your Instagram streams.

![set canvas resolution](https://images.wondershare.com/virbo/article/2024/03/guide-to-stream-to-instagram-with-rtmp-3.jpg)

* **Step 3.** After that, you need to add external sources to the scene. Click on the ‚Äú+‚Äù icon in the ‚ÄúScenes‚Äù panel or rename the scene. Once the scene is created, hit the ‚Äú+‚Äù icon of the ‚ÄúSources‚Äù panel. Then, select the ‚ÄúVideo Capture Device‚Äù option from the menu and rename the device to your liking. After that, choose the video capture device from the ‚ÄúDevice‚Äù dropdown and hit the ‚ÄúOK‚Äù option. Similarly, you can select the ‚ÄúAudio Input Capture‚Äù option to introduce an audio device.

![add a video capturing device](https://images.wondershare.com/virbo/article/2024/03/guide-to-stream-to-instagram-with-rtmp-4.jpg)

* **Step 4.** To add text overlays on your stream, hit the ‚Äú+‚Äù icon again in the ‚ÄúSources‚Äù panel. Choose the ‚ÄúText (GDI+)‚Äù option and rename your overlay. On the next window, insert your text in the ‚ÄúText‚Äù box. Then, click the ‚ÄúSelect Font‚Äù button and choose your required font style and size. Additionally, you can change font color, opacity, alignment, background, and more. After customizing the text, hit the ‚ÄúOK‚Äù button to save the overlay in the sources section.

![set up text properties](https://images.wondershare.com/virbo/article/2024/03/guide-to-stream-to-instagram-with-rtmp-5.jpg)

* **Step 5.** Afterward, select the ‚ÄúStream‚Äù option in the settings window of OBS Studio. Set the ‚ÄúService‚Äù to ‚ÄúCustom‚Äù from the dropdown. Then, you need to enter the server and stream key to proceed.
